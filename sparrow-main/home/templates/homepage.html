    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Sparrow and what is it used for?

    Sparrow is a comprehensive school management portal that enable schools automate school processes such as registration, attendance, examination and results, payments, meetings, and many other stuffs, which increases the school productivity and reduces the workload on the staff of the school.

    What do i benefit from using sparrow?

    There are numerous advantages of using sparrow school portals, some of which include automated and faster processes, more Secured data storage, easy data retrieval, and data visualization, Accurate insights and Predictions about most likely trend to occur and a whole lot more.

    Is it expensive to afford Sparrow?

    Sparrow is very affordable for schools, in fact you only pay for services you use. if later on you decide to add any extra feature that is not in the category you subscribe for, you can easily migrate your plan and you don't have to pay the full fee of the new plan, just a balance of the new fee.

    Do i get assistance when i want to set up my School?

    Absolutely! The system is easy to use and self explanatory, however there are still loads of support. we have a standby team for just that purpose that you can call or mail at any time of the day. There is also built in bot to assist you on that regard. if requested, we can do the full initialization for your school.

    Is sparrow user friendly and easy to use?

    Sparrow school portal is very user friendly and easy to you. you most likely would not require any form of external guidance to be able to easily flow with the software. However, if that need arises, you will still be well guided by the tech team until you familiarize yourself with the environment. The is also a manual for faster learning.

    Are my School Data safe with Sparrow?

    Yes. All data sent to sparrow are saved in secured cloud databases. They are also automatically backed up to avoid any form of data loss, also, sparrow would never check or use any data about your school without your permission. Any data you provide is exclusively for your consumption only.
